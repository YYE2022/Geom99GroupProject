Acquiring the Data

The data was obtained from the ArcGIS Rest Server for the Ministry of Environment and their MapServer. This is based off of Land Information Ontario (LIO) where the data containing the water quality as well as the watercourse and waterstations feature classes are located. This was the primary set data we used in conjunction with technologies we constructed to provide a solution
